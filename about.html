SHADE GARDENING

Shade gardening can be challenging for the novice gardener as many of the eye-catching plants in a garden need full sun to thrive. Even so, there are many annuals, perennials, and shrubs that can bring color to your landscape with both flowers and foliage. Annual plants last from spring until first frost while perennials return each spring.

This website is a quick reference guide of plants that will thrive in partial or full shade as recommended by Wingard's Nursery in Lexington, SC.
